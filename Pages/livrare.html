Delivery & Returns

Delivery and Order Pickup

Delivery in Moldova:

  • Home delivery in Chișinău: 77 MDL | 2-4 working days.
  • Delivery to other locations in Moldova: 87 MDL | 2-4 working days.
  • Urgent delivery by taxi: 120 MDL | within 24 hours.

International Delivery (Romania and England):

  • Delivery by minibus: €8 | 5-7 working days.

All orders are delivered via Nova Poshta courier. MIMILUX does not accept cash on delivery. All orders must be paid in advance by bank card or bank transfer.

In-Store Pickup:

  • Address: Gemenii Shopping Center, 2nd floor, Chișinău, Moldova.
  • Opening hours: Monday - Sunday, 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Payment methods available in-store: Cash, Bank card (Visa/Mastercard), Bank transfer.

Return and Exchange Policy

MIMILUX accepts returns or exchanges of products under the following conditions:

  • Non-customized products can be returned or exchanged within 14 calendar days from the purchase, provided they are unworn, in the original packaging, and without signs of wear.
  • Customized products (engraved with text, fingerprint, photo, logo, etc.) cannot be returned or exchanged, as they are made to the customer's request.

In case of manufacturing defects, MIMILUX offers repair, replacement, or refund, according to the warranty policy.
