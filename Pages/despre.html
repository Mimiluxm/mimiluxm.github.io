Mimilux Store is a personalized jewelry company, with an online and offline presence, specializing in engraved jewelry such as rings, necklaces, earrings, bracelets, and cufflinks.

The jewelry is made of stainless steel plated with 18-karat gold or rhodium, which makes it resistant to wear and fading. Mimilux offers a one-year warranty for each piece of jewelry, and engraving is included in the price. As for the packaging, the store offers over 10 types of packaging, with and without personalized messages, but the packaging is not included in the price of the jewelry.

Frequently asked questions

What materials do you use for jewelry?

Our jewelry is made of stainless steel plated with 18 karat gold or rhodium.

Do you offer customization options?

Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry to create something unique for you.

What is the return policy?

You can return non-personalized products within 30 days of purchase.

Can I order jewelry for a special event?

Of course! We accept orders for special events such as weddings, anniversaries or other important occasions.

Is your jewelry hypoallergenic?

Yes, most of our jewelry is made from hypoallergenic materials, ideal for sensitive skin.

Can I change the size of the jewelry?

Yes, we offer size adjustment services for most of our jewelry.

How long does it take to process a custom order?

The processing time for a custom order is approximately 7-14 business days.

Is the jewelry made from your own material?

Our jewelry is made using high-quality materials carefully selected from trusted suppliers. We ensure that they meet our standards for durability and aesthetics.
